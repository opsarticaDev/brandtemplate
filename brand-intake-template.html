Typography is the style of lettering used in your brand. Here are the main categories:

Elegant Boutique
Serif
Letters have small decorative lines (serifs) at the ends. Traditional, elegant, trustworthy. Think: Times New Roman, but more refined.
Modern Style
Sans-Serif
Clean letters without decorative lines. Modern, minimal, approachable. Think: The text you are reading right now.
Graceful Touch
Script / Decorative
Flowing, handwritten feel. Personal, artistic, feminine. Best used sparingly for accents.
